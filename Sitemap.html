Internal link connections
From
Recipes → Shop

Every recipe detail panel includes a "What I use for this" affiliate block linking directly to Shopee products.

From
About → Booking

The BookCTA section at the bottom of About links to the standalone 1:1 coaching booking calendar page.

From
Blog → Recipes

Recipe-category blog posts include a direct "See the full recipe" link to the Recipes page detail panel.

From
Blog → Shop

Nutrition and wellness posts that mention specific products link to the relevant section of the Shop page.

From
Home → All pages

The homepage hero, recipe grid, and shop grid all link out to their respective standalone pages for deep exploration.

From
Follow → TikTok / IG

The Follow page links out to both TikTok and Instagram profiles, driving external social platform growth.

From
Shop → Shopee VN

Every product "View on Shopee" button opens the affiliate link in a new tab — the primary affiliate revenue path.

From
Blog ✍️ → About

Personal story posts (like "How I Started") link to the full About Me page for deeper context on the creator's journey.
